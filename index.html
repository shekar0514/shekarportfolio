Post Covid Care

July-Aug 2022

The project focused on "Post COVID care" in Batlapalem village near Amalapuram. I conducted awareness sessions, interacting with villagers affected by COVID-19, discussing their precautions during the pandemic. I provided guidance on post-COVID precautions, including the installation of the "Arogya Setu app" on their mobiles. For those with short-term or long-term effects, tailored precautions were suggested. Self-quarantine at home or in hospitals was followed by individuals to prevent virus spread. Additionally, I advised affected individuals to consider medication, yoga, proper rest, and avoiding overexertion to aid in recovery.
